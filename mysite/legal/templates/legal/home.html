Apostilles, Authentications, and Legalizations

At The Patent Place we have been helping our associates apostille, authenticate, & legalize documents for 25+ years. We are well trained to handle documents for countries that are and are not apart of the Hague Convention. Please follow the instructions below to obtain a cost and time estimate and to place your order with us!

Cost & Time Estimates:

  • Number of documents: Select the number of documents you need legalized. Discounts are added to orders with multiple documents that originated in the same location and are being legaized for the same country.
  • Document Type
    1. Notarized/True copy: Notarized copy of document (i.e., power of attorney, deed of assignment, etc.…),
    2. Certified state document: A Certified Document obtained from the State within the last 60 days (i.e., article’s of incorporation, certificate of existence, etc.…)
    3. Certified USPTO document: A document certified by the US Patent and Trademark Office (i.e., patent, patent assignment, patent application, etc.…)
  • Provider of Document: Are you (the client) providing the document or would you like to order through The Patent Place, Inc? (clients must provide notarized documents)
  • State: where the document originated or was notarized (not applicable for certified USPTO documents).
  • Country: Where the document is needed.
  • Copies: Color copies of your completed documents can be e-mailed upon request.
