File Types

  • File History (wrapper)
    • A complete record of the application, including all of the documents relating to the patent such as diagrams and drawings; communications between the USPTO & the applicant; statements made by the applicant; and records of official actions by the USPTO.
  • Application
    • A copy of the original application as filed. Please note orders for copy of applications will not have any papers that were filed after the intial applicaiton.
  • Assignments
    • Document showing the owner of the intellectual property.
  • Non-Patent Literature (NPL)
    • Documents and publications that are not patents or published patent applications, but are cited as references for being relevant in a patent prosecution. Examples: A magazine article or a doctoral thesis.
